Matt Furie introduced an alternate persona named "Young Peezy" for Pepe in the "Boys Club" comic series, where Pepe originated. Surprisingly, this persona went unnoticed by cryptocurrency enthusiasts until now. Young Peezy makes appearances in pages 142, 143, and 154 of the comic.
$PEEZY is the first meme coin in crypto to bring the Young Peezy character to life as Pepe’s cool alternate persona. $PEPE and $PEEZY are the only two Pepe coins carrying official names from the comic.
